Help & User Guide

📥 How to Request Dummy Units

1
Click the + Request Unit button at the top right.
2
Select your Test Department (Test 1 or Test 2).
3
Enter your WBI — your name will auto-fill.
4
Select the package you need from the dropdown.
5
Enter the Handler Number of the machine (e.g. MX94).
6
Fill in the Purpose and click Submit Request.
7
Your request will be reviewed by admin. Check Queued List for status.

📤 How to Return Dummy Units

1
Go to Main Dashboard and find your request in the Borrowed List.
2
Search by your Handler Number if needed.
3
Click the Return button on your row.
4
Enter your WBI and fill in the unit condition breakdown.
5
Click Confirm Return. Stock will be updated automatically.

📋 How to Track Your Request

1
Click Queued List in the left menu.
2
Find your request by REQ number or handler number.
3
Status: Pending (waiting for admin), Active (approved), Rejected.

⚠️ Important Reminders

!
Always enter the correct handler number when requesting.
!
Return dummy units promptly after PM cycle completion.
!
Report any missing units immediately to your supervisor and admin.
!
Units overdue more than 14 days will be flagged in the system.
